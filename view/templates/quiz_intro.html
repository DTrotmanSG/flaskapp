Track Matching Assessment

Welcome to the UBS Talent Accelerator. This assessment helps match you to the track that best aligns with your skills, interests, and career goals. There are no “right” or “wrong” answers — we’re simply looking to understand your profile.

The assessment takes approximately 10–15 minutes and covers five key areas:

  • Skills & Experience — your technical background and exposure
  • Technical Aptitude — how you approach problem‑solving
  • Work Style — your preferred way of working
  • Career Motivation — what drives your career path
  • Personality Fit — your communication and collaboration style

Be authentic in your responses. This assessment is designed to help you find the best track fit.
